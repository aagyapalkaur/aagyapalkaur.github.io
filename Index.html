Project Outline:

In this contemporary era of digitization, massive amount of data is generated everyday through social media platforms, health care systems, education, finance sector, IT industries and many more. And this huge sets of data is called Big Data. As the volume, variety and velocity of data is increasing day by day to a great extent, so, it is very arduous to analyse or deal with such large scale datasets. Clustering is an essential data mining tool that plays an important role for analysing big data. However, large scale data clustering has become a challenging task because of the large amount of information that emerges from technological progress in many areas, including finance and business informatics. Map Reduce is one of the most famous frameworks, way of handling and processing huge amount of data in context of many complex and large-scale real-life applications. This paper presents the approach of a parallel clustering algorithm based on Map Reduce paradigm to be able to perform clustering on large-scale of data.
